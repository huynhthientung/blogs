Welcome to my blog.

I'm Tung — a software developer based in Vietnam. This is where I write about things I build, ideas I'm exploring, and lessons I pick up along the way.

## What to expect

- **Deep-dives** into topics I'm actively working with
- **Notes** on software architecture and engineering practices
- **Thoughts** on tools, workflows, and developer experience

I write primarily for myself — to solidify understanding and have something to reference later. If it's useful to you too, that's a bonus.

## The stack

This blog is built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com), deployed to GitHub Pages, and lives at `blogs.huynhthientung.com`. Posts are plain Markdown files in a Git repo — no CMS, no database, no nonsense.

## Why write at all?

> Writing is thinking. To write well is to think clearly. That's why it's so hard.
>
> — David McCullough

I've found that the act of writing forces precision. Vague ideas that seem solid in your head fall apart when you try to explain them. Writing is the test.
